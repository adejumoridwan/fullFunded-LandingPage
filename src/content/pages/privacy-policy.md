## 1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information to provide our services effectively.

### 1.1 Personal Data You Provide

- Name, email address, and contact information when you sign up or subscribe

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Data

- IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
- Pages visited, time spent on pages, and interaction data
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

### 1.3 Data from Third Parties

- Publicly available information about principal investigators and funding programs for our curated listings
- Analytics and advertising data from third-party services (Google Analytics, etc.) to improve site performance

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery** – Provide access to fully funded programs, postdoc opportunities, and verified PI contacts
- **Account Management** – Manage your account, subscription, and user preferences
- **Communication** – Respond to inquiries, send updates, newsletters, and relevant research opportunities
- **Analytics & Improvements** – Analyze user behavior to improve our platform and offerings
- **Legal Compliance** – Fulfill obligations under applicable laws and regulations

We process personal data only where necessary and based on your consent, contractual necessity, or legitimate interest.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, we rely on one or more of the following lawful bases:

- **Consent:** You agree to receive communications or personalized content
- **Contract:** Processing is necessary to provide our subscription services
- **Legal Obligation:** Compliance with laws
- **Legitimate Interest:** To improve our platform and ensure service functionality, without adversely affecting your rights

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers:** Payment processors, email services, cloud hosting, and analytics platforms
- **Academic Networks:** Publicly available PI contacts for research opportunities
- **Legal Authorities:** If required by law or to protect our legal rights

All third-party partners are GDPR-compliant and obligated to protect your data.

## 5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Personalize content and improve user experience
- Analyze site usage and performance
- Facilitate login sessions and account management

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect functionality.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to:

- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 7. Your Rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

- **Access** – Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification** – Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure** – Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict Processing** – Limit the processing of your data
- **Data Portability** – Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Object** – Object to processing for marketing or legitimate interest purposes
- **Withdraw Consent** – At any time for data processing based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at ridwan@fullyfunded.online. We will respond within one month, as required by GDPR.

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be stored or processed outside your country. We ensure that any international transfer of data is compliant with GDPR requirements, using adequate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.
